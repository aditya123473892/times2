Our Services

Learning Tools

Interactive tools like Smart board, Smart Projectors, Panels, etc. coupled with interactive content and tools have taken the learning experience to a different level. It has transformed the Class rooms. Even the way we learn language is re-defined.
While it improves the learning, it helps the teachers deliver better results with lower stress.

Management Tools

From a register-based management to an Education ERP it has seen a sea change. All the functions of the Education starting from Admission to Fees collection to Transport management to Examination are transformed. Even the parent side interaction has changed for the better.

Green Computing

Over 70% of the computing power produced is not utilized. Over 50% of the Electricity used by computing devices are wasted and simply creates heat.
Our green computing initiative works towards the optimization of computing infrastructure for better utilization. We also help save up to 42% power on computers.

Secured Cyberway

Management of the Institution is responsible for the Internet Bandwidth and is directly responsible for any misuse. Managing Internet Bandwidth, Content, and Usage ensuring security is not easy. With a large number of students, it becomes even more challenging.
TIMES IT SOLUTIONS integrates robust endpoint – Network -, Application – Gateway security with proven credentials.

Simplified Info-infra

Institutions require an infrastructure that can be easily managed and anyone can take charge of. All this while it ensures proper delivery all the time. We help streamline the IT infrastructure starting from better networks, to Audits, Mail management, Asset Management, etc.
We integrate Surveillance for further integration with the IT framework.

Specialized Licensing & Training

We work out very special deals for the institutions for Special Education Price Licensing.
Our training division conducts special training courses on Security, ITIL, Green Computing, etc, and acts as a finishing school for better success.

M-Governance

Mobile has revolutionized the space of field governance and monitoring. Combined with the power of precise GPS mapping, Mobile data capturing, cloud computing, and database analytics and it creates a great M-Governance.
Our expertise lies in developing scalable solutions and delivering in record timings.

E-Governance

“Minimum Government Maximum Governance” is the keyword. Ensuring that the benefits reach the last man.We specialize in Social Benefit Transfer, Education Delivery system, and such specialized processes for E-Governance.
